About Andi

Why We’re the Best

We're not here to reinvent food, just to make it unforgettable. At Andi’s Honest Work, every dish has a purpose: to make you smile. No trends, no tricks. Just honest work.

Our Story

Andi grew up in Dalian, by the sea, where she learned two things early: how to spot fresh fish and how to eat them faster than anyone else. Her curiosity for food led her across Tokyo, Paris, and the colder parts of Europe : each place adding something to her plate and personality.

This is her honest work. And maybe her best work, too.
